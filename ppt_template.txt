# PPT Template: Global Energy Transition Dashboard Project

## Slide 1: Title & Project Overview
- **Title:** Global Energy Transition: Predicting the Future of Renewables
- **Subtitle:** An End-to-End Data Analytics and Machine Learning Dashboard
- **Key Highlights:**
    - Objective: Predicting `renewables_share_energy` using global macroeconomic and energy data.
    - Technology Stack: Python, Pandas, Scikit-Learn, Streamlit, Plotly.
    - Dataset: OWID Energy Data (23k+ records).

## Slide 2: The Data Journey (Preprocessing & Cleaning)
- **Challenge:** Handling a "dirty" dataset with huge size and massive missing values.
- **The Transformation:**
    - **Row Reduction:** 23,232 rows → 4,325 rows (Focusing on country-level data and non-null targets).
    - **Leakage Prevention:** Identified and removed 80+ "cheat" columns (e.g., specific solar/wind shares).
    - **Cleaning Techniques:**
        - Median Imputation for missing values (preserving distribution).
        - Isolation Forest for robust Outlier Handling.
        - Statistical filtering (dropping features with corr > 0.8 to avoid multicollinearity).

## Slide 3: Actionable Insights (EDA)
- **Global Snapshot:** Visualizing current renewable energy shares using an interactive Choropleth Map (ISO-code based).
- **Macro Dynamics:**
    - Animated Scatter Plots: Visualizing the relationship between GDP growth and Electricity Generation over time (2000-2022).
    - Country Trends: In-depth analysis of transition trajectories for specific nations.
- **Future Projections:** Using CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate) logic to forecast energy ratios to 2030.

## Slide 4: Feature Selection & Model Performance
- **Optimizing Compute:** Showcasing how dimensionality reduction cut training time while maintaining high accuracy.
- **Strategies Compared:**
    - Filter-Based (SelectKBest)
    - Wrapper-Based (Sequential Feature Selector)
    - Embedded (Lasso L1 Regularization)
    - Feature Extraction (PCA)
- **Best Model:** Random Forest and Bagging Regressors achieved R² scores > 0.95 with optimized feature sets.

## Slide 5: The Prediction Lab & Future Outlook
- **Interactive Simulator:** A "Sandbox" where users can tweak variables manually to see real-time impact on renewable predictions.
- **The "Green Meter":**
    - Categorizing predictions into stages: Fossil Dependent (<10%), Transitioning (10-40%), and Green Leader (>40%).
- **Conclusion:** Data-driven insights suggest a significant global shift toward renewables, provided macroeconomic factors stay favorable.
